The Basics

The Pomodoro technique is a time-management strategy where, for 25 minutes at a time, you focus solely on a task you need to get done, with nothing to interrupt you. A 5 minute break, where one can use one's phone, or derp around on the intertent, is generally allotted between intervals. Working in this way helps with productivity while effectively reducing burn-out.
